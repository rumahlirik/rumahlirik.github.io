6. 12. "... Jobs became an instant billionaire." The underlined word is similar in meaning to A. complete B. immediate C. responsible D. wealthy E. humble​


~B. INGGRIS~

question:

"... Jobs became an instant billionaire."

Kata yang digarisbawahi mirip artinya dengan...

answer:

Yang digarisbawahi (mungkin) adalah kata instant. Di mana kata tersebut jika diartikan ke dalam bahasa Indonesia akan memiliki arti yang sama. Jadi, kata yang mirip dengan instant adalah:

B. immediate

Arti kata immediate adalah secepatnya. Jadi, kata tersebut merupakan sinonim dari instant.

12. rangkunlah alur cerita film The Billionaire​


Berikut rangkuman alur cerita dari film dengan judul The Billionaire:

Di usianya yang ke-16 tahun, tokoh utama dalam film ini dikisahkan sebagai pencandu game online. Saat ia beranjak pada usia ke-17, ia pun resmi putus sekolah dan memutuskan menjadi penjual kacang. Ketika berusia 18 tahun, keluargany dinyatakan bangkrut dengan utang sebesar 40 juta baht. Lalu kemudian di usia 19 tahun, sosok tokoh utama berhasil menciptakan produk dari rumput laut dengan nama Tao Kae Noi. Produk ini digemari dan berhasil terjual sebanyak 3.000 buah di semua cabang 7-Eleven yang ada di Thailand. Saat usianya menginjak 26 Tahun, tokoh utama berhasil menduduki posisi sebagai produsen cemilan berbahan rumput laut terbesar dan terkaya di Thailand. Diyakini penghasilannya mencapai 800 juta baht dengan total pekerja 2.000 orang. Tokoh utama dalam film ini adalah Top Ittipat yang adalah sosok nyata yang memang menjadi salah satu sosok inspiratif di Thailand.Pembahasan

If you were a billionaire and wanted to start a business, there are several steps you can consider:

1. Conduct research: Before starting a business, it is essential to research the market you are planning to enter. 2. Define your goals: Determine your long-term and short-term goals by defining what you want to achieve with your business. It can be profits, social recognition, personal satisfaction, or a combination of these factors.

3. Create a business plan: A business plan outlines the steps you need to take to establish and run your business successfully. It should include details about your business model, funding, marketing strategy, and operational plan.

4. Secure funding: With your substantial financial resources, you can opt to self-finance or seek external funding. You can approach angel investors, venture capitalists, or banks for financing. Ensure that you have a solid business plan and pitch to secure funding.

5. Hire a team: Identify your leadership team, employees, and supporting staff that are suitable for your business structure. Hiring the right people can help you achieve your goals and provide support in different aspects of your business.

6. Launch and market: After completing all the above steps, launch your business and focus on marketing strategies to generate awareness about your product/service. Utilize various channels, including social media, email marketing, and in-person advertising, to reach your target customers.

Remember, starting a business requires hard work, dedication, and commitment. By following the above steps, you can create a strong foundation for your business and increase your chances of success.

Passive Voice:

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

40. Which statement belongs to conditional sentence type 3? a. If you invest your money on my startup, you will be a billionaire within two years. b. Stephanie won't be able to sleep, if she drinks this coffee. c. If you happy, you look amazing d. If you had bought the tickets, we would have watched the concert. e. If you heat water, it boils.


Jawaban:

To make a sentence in the third conditional, we use,

If + past perfect, would/wouldn't have + past participle.

So, the answer is D

Penjelasan:

Conditional sentence type 3 digunakan untuk pengandaian jika suatu hal terjadi di masa lalu maka kemungkinan akan menimbulkan dampak Yang berbeda dengan kenyataan Sekarang.

contohnya:

If you had bought the tickets, we would have watched the concert. = Jika kamu telah membeli tiketnya, kita pasti sudah menonton konser itu.

Kalimat di atas merupakan bentuk pengandaian, yakni andaikan YOU membeli tiket, maka WE menonton konser. Sedangkan pada kenyataannya mereka tidak menonton konser Karena YOU tidak membeli tiket.
