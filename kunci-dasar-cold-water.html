11. if we put hot water in cold water it will manifest ??​


Penjelasan:

Saat mencampur air panas dengan air dingin, kita harus mengaduknya.

Ada yang tahu kenapa?

Lebih berat

Air panas dan air dingin memiliki berat yang berbeda.

Air dingin lebih berat daripada air panas.

Jadi, saat digabungkan, air dingin akan berada di bawah dan air panas akan berada di atas.

Agar menjadi air hangat dalam waktu singkat, kita harus mengaduknya.

Jawaban:

When you mix hot water with cold water, you have to stir it.

Anyone know why?

Heavier

Hot water and cold water have different weights.

Cold water is heavier than hot water.

So when combined, cold water will be on the bottom and hot water will be on top.

In order for it to become warm water in no time, we have to stir it.

She takes a bath with cold water every morningThe interrogative form of the sentence is ...a. does she take a-bath with cold water every morning?b. does she takes a bath with cold water every morning?c. do she takes a bath with cold water every morning?d." do she take a bath with cold water every morning?​


The interrogative form of the sentence is “does she take a-bath with cold water every morning?“

Jawabannya adalah A ✔️

PEMBAHASAN Simple Present Tense

➡️ Digunakan utk menunjukkan suatu Kegiatan atau Peristiwa yg saat ini (Present) atau sebuah kebiasaan/rutinitas dan kebenaran umum atau FAKTA.

➡️ Adverb of time

EverydayEvery morningUsuallyRegularly

➡️ Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + VERB 1 (-s/es) (-) S + DO/DOES + not + VERB 1 (?) DO/DOES + S + VERB 1?

➡️ Kalimat Present Tense

(+) She takes a bath with cold water every morning(-) she does not take a bath with cold water every morning?(?) does she take a-bath with cold water every morning? ✔️

KESIMPULAN Yang ditanyakan adalah kalimat Interrogative Atau kalimat tanya maka diawali dengan kata DOES dan menggunakan VERB-1
