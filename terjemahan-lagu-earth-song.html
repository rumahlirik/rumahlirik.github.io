3. tolong buatin REASON untuk lagu earth song-michel jakson


Earth Song tackles the theme of the care of theenvironment, the text deals with war,pollution, deforestation and animal cruelty. Jackson is trying to pass an environmental message, he believes that the song is an opportunity for people to hear the "voice of the planet" (« I think earth feels the pain, and she has wounds […] this is my chance to pretty much let people hear the voice of the planet. »)
