Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: gap fill

This sentence has some alternate answers. For example:
- wait a minute is a tag on my bag.
- wait a minute is something I say when I see someone steals my bag.
- wait a minute is a title of a book kept in my bag.
