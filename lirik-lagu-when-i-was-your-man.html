9. Let's kuis dalam rangka naik rank. +30 poin"and when your father turns to stone" Kalimat diatas merupakan bagian lagu... (sertakan potongan lirik & makna lagunya) ​


Jawaban:

"and when your father turns to stone"

The sentence is part of a song called House of Gold by Twenty One Pilots.

Below is just some part of the lyrics:

She asked me, Son, when I grow old

Will you buy me a house of gold?

And when your father turns to stone

Will you take care of me?

So, the meaning of the song is that he (the singer) wants to take care of his mother when his father passed away (turned to stone). Not only that the son (the singer) wants to buy his mother a house of gold in order to fulfill her wish, but also more than that.

Penjelasan:

Judul lagunya adalah House of Gold by Twenty One Pilots.

Jadi, makna dari lagu tersebut adalah dia (penyanyi) ingin merawat ibunya ketika ayahnya meninggal (berubah menjadi batu). Bukan hanya sang putra (penyanyi) ingin membelikan ibunya sebuah rumah emas untuk memenuhi keinginannya saja, tetapi juga lebih dari itu.

Semoga membantu ya.
