PEMBAHASAN Past Tense

➡️ Digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang terjadi di masa lampau.

➡️ Cirinya:

yesterday,two days agolast night ✔️

➡️ Rumusnya:

(+) S + Verb-2 + Complement(-) S + didn’t + Verb-1 + not+ Complement(?) Did + S + Verb-1 + Complement?

KESIMPULAN

Alina SANG a song on my birthday party last night

Keterangan:

kalimat past Tense “last night” maka menggunakan Verb-2

Verb-1 “sing”Verb-2 “sang” ✔️Verb-3 "sung"

29. mary (talk)............to jhon on the phone last night.​


Mary talked to Jhon on the phone last night.

-

Pendahuluan

Simple Past Tense digunakan untuk berbicara tentang aktivitas masa lalu. Simple Past sendiri biasanya menggunakan past verb/verb 2. Keterangan waktu yang biasa digunakan :

Last weekLast nightYesterdayetc.

-

Formula—Verbal

(+) S + Verb 2 + o/c

(-) S + did not + Verb 1 + o/c

(?) Did + S + verb 1 + o/c ?

—Nominal

(+) S + to be (was/were) + Adj/Adv/Noun

(-) S + to be (was/were) + not + Adj/Adv/Noun

(?) To be (was/were) + S + Adj/Adv/Noun

Catatan penting!Was⇒I, she, he, itWere⇒you, we, they, we

-

PembahasanSoal

Mary (talk) to Jhon on the phone last week.

KeteranganMary⇒SubjectTalk⇒Verb 1On the phone last week⇒O/CJawaban

Mary talked to Jhon on the phone last week.

Penjelasan Mary↣SubjectTalked↣Verb 2On the phone last night
