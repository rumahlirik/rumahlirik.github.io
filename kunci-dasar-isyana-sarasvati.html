Isyana Sarasvati is an Indonesian pop, R&B and Jazz singer. He became known after releasing the second single titled "Remain in the Soul" in 2015. Isyana's childhood and youth were full of musical competitions. Since childhood, the child of the couple Sapta Dwikardana and Luana Marpanda has been accustomed to being quarantined in preparation for music concerts. Exercise can be 8 to 12 hours per day. At the age of 9, Isyana took part in the Electone Festival and was immediately the champion in West Java and became a national finalist for the National Yamaha Piano Competition. His ability continues to be honed until he is a teenager in the competition. Thanks to his hard work, he also won several trophies. He won the Asia Pacific Electone Festival Grand Prix three times in 2005, 2008 and 2011. In the midst of being serious about participating in various competitions, he tries to express his singing through the YouTube channel.

Isyana uses social media to make her dream come true through a cover music video on the YouTube channel. He performed the song I Will Fly from Ten 2 Five and Rather Be from Clean Bandit. Isayana always performs other musicians' songs with his own character.

Not only good at singing, Isyana is also very good at writing songs and playing musical instruments. He even became the composer for his first single entitled Keep Being You. This song was released in 2014 when he was studying music at the Nanyang Academy of Fine Arts Singapore.

After a year, the singer who is adept at playing the piano, flute and saxophone released his second single titled "Stay in the Soul". This song succeeded in catapulting his name in the Indonesian music scene.

The song "Remain in the Soul" is based on the true story of Isyana's friend abroad whose relationship story fell apart due to differences. According to Isyana, the song was a bestseller because the story of a broken heart is something that is universal and can happen to anyone.

Also in 2015, Isyana released her first album titled "Explore!" with 10 track songs in it. Isyana's name became even more famous as an Indonesian singer when she was 22 years old. His work also widened into the world of advertising. He was asked to endorse several large advertisements.

Despite venturing into other worlds, Isyana remains consistent with the world that has raised her name. In 2016, the singer graduated from the Royal College of Music, England,
released the album “Explore! Special Edition".

The album contains 1 new single composed by Isyana herself and several of her old songs, rearranged. Her professional career was also rewarded with the Female Singer of the Year & Album of the Year award, NET. Indonesian Choice Awards 2016.

In 2017, he returned to work. Isyana collaborated with singer Raisa on the song "Anganku Anganmu". Isyana is also increasingly famous as a top singer who hopes that many people will remember her work. (AC/DN)
