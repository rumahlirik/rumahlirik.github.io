4. The song is called "Dear Mama" by Tupac Shakur. The message of the song is about the rapper's love and appreciation for his mother and how she has always been there for him through thick and thin. The song is a tribute to all mothers who have sacrificed so much for their children.

5. The verse is the part of the song where the lyrics are spoken rather than sung. In "Dear Mama," the verses are the parts where Tupac raps about his childhood and his mother's struggles.

The chorus is the part of the song that is repeated after each verse. In "Dear Mama," the chorus is the part where Tupac sings "Mama I love you, Mama I care, Mama I love you, Mama my friend."

6. The line "Every other day I crossed the line I didn't mean to be so bad" is an example of hyperbole, which is a figure of speech that uses exaggeration to make a point. In this case, Tupac is exaggerating how often he got into trouble as a child.

1. (+) = I Want you.

(-) = I Don't Want you.

(?) = Do i want you? (Bisa juga menjadi Do you want me?).

2. (+) = She likes you.

(-) = She doesn't Like you.

(?) = Does she like you?

3. (+) I am the only one you love.

(-) I am not the only one you love.

(?) Am i the only one you love?

4. (+) He is interested in loving somebody.

(-) He is not Interested in loving somebody.

(?) Is he interested in loving somebody?

40. GABUNGKAN KALIMAT INI DENGAN BOTH, EITHER, ATAU NEITHER 1. I don’t like MotoGP. I don’t like formula 1 ( neither ) 2. They want to go to Mall. They want to go to cinema ( either ) 3. We will spend our holiday in Bali. We will spend our holiday in Lombok ( both ) 4. You like coffee. You like tea ( both ) 5. My sister spoke english. My sister spoke bahasa ( either ) TOLONG YANG NGERTI AJA


▪ Correlative Conjunction

I neither like MotoGP nor Formula 1. They want either to go to the mall or the cinema. We will spend our holiday both in Bali and Lombok. You like both coffee and tea. My sister spoke either English or Bahasa.

Silakan simak penjelasan berikut.

PENDAHULUAN

Correlative conjunction – atau jika pada bahasa Indonesia, disebut dengan konjungsi berpasangan – merupakan sepasang konjungsi (kata penghubung) yang berguna untuk menyambungkan dua kata, klausa, atau frasa sehingga menjadi kalimat yang padu.

Correlative conjunction terdiri dari:

Neither - norEither - orWhether - orNot only - but alsoBoth - andEtc.

PEMBAHASAN

Perintah soalnya adalah menyatukan kalimat berikut dengan correlative conjunction di dalam kurung pada setiap nomor. Berikut jawaban dan penjelasannya.

1. I don’t like MotoGP. I don’t like formula 1 (neither).

➥ I neither like MotoGP nor Formula 1.

Aku tidak suka MotoGP dan Formula 1.

2. They want to go to Mall. They want to go to cinema (either).

➥ They want either to go to the mall or the cinema.

Mereka mau pergi ke mall ataupun ke bioskop.

3. We will spend our holiday in Bali. We will spend our holiday in Lombok (both).

➥ We will spend our holiday both in Bali and Lombok.

Kami akan berlibur ke Bali dan Lombok.

4. You like coffee. You like tea (both).

➥ You like both coffee and tea.

Kamu suka kopi dan teh.

5. My sister spoke english. My sister spoke bahasa (either).

➥ My sister spoke either English or Bahasa.

Kakak/adik peremuanku bisa bicara bahasa Inggris ataupun bahasa Indonesia.

KESIMPULAN

Jawaban dari soalnya adalah:

I neither like MotoGP nor Formula 1. They want either to go to the mall or the cinema. We will spend our holiday both in Bali and Lombok. You like both coffee and tea. My sister spoke either English or Bahasa.
