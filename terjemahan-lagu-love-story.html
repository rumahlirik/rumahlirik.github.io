1. pesan singkat lagu love story bahasa Inggris serta terjemahan.tolong kak dikumpulkan hari ini ​


Jawaban:

After we interpret and interpret the entire lyrics of the song, it is clear that the song "Love Story" tells about the story of Romeo and Juliet. Unlike the original story which tends to be full of drama and ends tragically because the couple committed suicide, the story of this song seems to have a happy ending. Where the songwriters seem to want to improve the story of Romeo and Juliet by writing down what would happen if they did not commit suicide. It is clear from the lyrics, after they did not commit suicide, they faced all these problems and ran away. Even though his father opposed him, they continued to hold on to their love belief until they were finally blessed. They finally got married, became kings and queens and lived happily ever after. The lesson we can learn from this song is that what is our belief, as long as it is true, then fight for it. Because love is not forced, because love is a beautiful thing that should be the happiest story, not even ending tragically.

9. majas/figure of speech lagu love story by Taylor Swift JAWAB YANG BENER NO NGASAL​


Jawaban:

This study focused on Figurative Language and message that used on "Love Story" song by Taylor Swift. The purposes of this research are (1) to know the figurative Language in "Love Story" song by Taylor Swift and (2) to know the message from "Love Story" song by Taylor Swift. This research was a qualitative research design because the writer is easier to present the result of the collected data and it was intended to know the figurative language and message on "Love Story" song by Taylor Swift. In addition, the instrument that used in this research is the writer. The result of this research showed that the lyrics on the song have many expressions such as sad, angry, jealous and many more expressions. The figurative languages that used in this song are metaphor, irony, sycnodoche, symbol and hyperbole. The most dominat figure of speech here is metaphor. Moreover, the general message in "Love Story" song is we should try hard to get something that we want, no matter how difficult the obstacles. We should always believe that there is no result that betrays the process.

 BAHASA INDONESIA:

“Love Story” menceritakan tentang kisa Romeo dan Juliet. Berbeda dengan kisah aslinya yang cenderung penuh drama dan berakhir tragis karna pasangan tersebut bunuh diri, kisah pada lagu ini seolah-olah berakhir dengan bahagia. Dimana penulis lagu seolah-olah ingin memperbaiki kisah Romeo dan Juliet dengan menuliskan apa yang akan terjadi jika keduanya tidak bunuh diri. Terlihat jelas pada lirik, setelah mereka tidak bunuh diri, mereka menghadapi semua masalah tersebut dan melarikan diri. Meskipun ayahnya menentangnya, mereka terus mempertahankan keyakinan cintanya tersebut sampai akhirnya direstui. Merekapun akhirnya menikah, menjadi raja dan ratu dan hidup bahagia selamanya. Pelajaran yang dapat kita petik dari lagu ini bahwa apa yang menjadi keyakinan kita, asal semua itu benar, maka perjuangkanlah. Karena cinta itu bukan karna paksaan, karna cinta itu adalah hal indah yang seharusnya menjadi kisah yang paling bahagia, bukan malah berakhir tragis.
